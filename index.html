Our Mission

Acknowledging the current lackluster state of the New Player Experience, 2RAF has chosen to take upon itself the training of newly arrived combatants for the Terran Republic and harden their Spirit in Coordinated Battle upon the lands of Auraxis. We who lead the outfit, have each been chosen for our continuous desire to help people and see them thrive as we conquer in the name of Dakka.

Our Statement

Train our members to thrive in battle.

Our Vision

Become one of the top training outfits of Emerald TR.

Community

The community has shown to be one of comradeship, unity and a gathered sense that we are all part of something greater than ourselves. It is a place many people can escape to when they have a few hours on their hands to fight for the glory of the Terran Republic on Auraxis, doing so by enforcing and adhering to the values as stated in our Code of Conduct.

Our Promise

Maintain the Healthy & Welcoming State of the Community, offering members an escape to which they wish to return.

Code of Conduct

Our code of conduct focuses on a few core values: remember the human (both friend and foe), play for the enjoyment of the game, and above all else, be a positive representation of the 2RAF community. If you’d like to know more, click here to view our full Code of Conduct.
